Welcome! We're excited you're interested in starting a Civic Tech Project. Depending on the maturity of your idea, you'll join either the **Onboarding Track** or the **Incubator Track** — more on that below.

But first, let's check your eligibility.

## Eligibility

We welcome projects from:

- **Nonprofits & community organizations** — digitizing paper processes, building tools, or creating proofs-of-concept that make a community impact.
- **Local government** — community feedback tools, service request systems, or new ways government can engage constituents.
- **Activists & community members** — if you're close to a problem, you likely know it best. We want to hear your ideas.

Don't fit neatly into one of these? You're still welcome to pitch.

#### What we don't support

- **Electoral work** — Civic Tech DC does not run campaigns or endorse candidates. We are strictly nonpartisan. Advocacy is welcome, but outputs must serve the public interest and be usable across party lines.
- **Startups** — eligible only for civic-focused projects that meet all project requirements.

## Becoming a Project

Your project must meet all four criteria:

| | Criteria | Details |
|---|---|---|
| ✔ | **It's civic tech** | The project must relate to civic tech in some meaningful way. *(See definition)* |
| ✔ | **It's open source** | Open source lets volunteers contribute. All projects live on our GitHub *(civictechdc/civictechdc-website)* |
| ✔ | **It benefits a real community or partner** | A clear end user or validator ensures the project addresses an actual need. |
| ✔ | **You're committed as project lead** | All projects are volunteer-run. You (or a teammate) must be available to define scope and guide volunteers. Timelines are flexible — we'll help you scope to fit your availability. |

#### Where you land depends on your answers

- ✅ **Yes to all four** — You're ready for the **Onboarding Track!**
- ❌ **No to civic tech or open source** — Unfortunately, we can't accept the project.
- ❌ **No to community benefit** — You'll start in the **Incubator Track** while we help develop that piece.
- ❌ **No to project lead** — No problem! You can still pitch your idea and participate as an **Advisor**.
